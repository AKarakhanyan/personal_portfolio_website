About

Since I can remember, I've been passionate about making an impact on the world. Through that lens I discovered neuroscience research. I've loved the journey, even with its downs. Working in research gave me 6 years of best-in-class problem solving, critical thinking and resilience when working towards a goal.

I needed to do more, I needed to see the impact of my work and so I decided to leave research to pursue web development. In research, you would work weeks before seeing the result of your experiment. However with web development, I can work a few hours and be able to create a prototype or make significant progress on an application. That is life changing! I have not looked back since.
